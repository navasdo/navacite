Welcome to Navacite

Navacite is a collection of web-based tools for Speech-Language Pathologists, shaped by the shared expertise of special educators, authors, and clinicians. These resources provide engaging, no-prep digital materials to support therapy for a wide range of communication goals.

Language Tools

Target core language skills with interactive apps that focus on everything from syntax and grammar to reading comprehension and social language.

Interactive Language Apps

Engage students with dynamic tools like Grammar Grid and Cochlea Compass. These apps help build foundational skills in sentence structure, written expression, listening comprehension, and executive functioning through hands-on practice.

  • Syntax & Grammar
  • Listening Comprehension
  • Written Expression

Fluency Resources

Support students who stutter with tools and hierarchies designed for avoidance reduction therapy, fluency shaping, and building confident communication.

Stuttering Modification Tools

Engage students with interactive materials that help them explore and understand the mechanics of speech, identify moments of disfluency without fear, and practice techniques in a supportive environment.

Articulation Card Decks

Access a comprehensive library of digital flashcards for every phoneme in the English language, perfect for drill-based practice and generalization.

Comprehensive Phoneme Library

Each phoneme features extensive stimulus lists, from single words across all positions and syllable structures to complex phrases, sentences, and reading passages. This structured approach supports motor learning from isolation to conversation.

SLP Clerical Tools

Streamline your workflow with utilities built to handle the administrative tasks of a practicing clinician, saving you time for what matters most.

Session Scribe

Transform your shorthand therapy notes into professional, compliant documentation. This tool helps you quickly generate structured notes that are ready for inclusion in official records, ensuring consistency and accuracy.
